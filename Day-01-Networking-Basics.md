# Day 01 - Networking Fundamentals

## Topics Covered
- Networking basics
- Data transfer rate
- Client-server model
- Types of networks

## Explanation
Networking connects devices to share data.

Data transfer rate decides how fast information travels.

The client-server model explains how clients request and servers respond.

Different networks include LAN, MAN, WAN, and PAN.

## Why This Matters in Cybersecurity
All attacks and defenses happen through networks.

## My Key Takeaway
Networking is the backbone of cybersecurity.
